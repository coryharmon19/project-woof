The Bulldog

Bulldogs are the fourth most popular dog breed ever. They are loved by so many people because they are a calm, courageous, and friendly breed. Bulldogs normally have a medium amount of energy and are a medium sized dog. They have a smooth coat with a heavy, thick, low swung body. It has a massive short faced head, wide shoulders, and sturdy limbs.

Bulldogs require minimal grooming and exercise. However, they are greedy eaters, and will overeat if given the chance. They cannot tolerate extreme heat and humidity. The bulldog is a lover, not a fighter, making it an excellent companion for kids.

  • Life span of 8-10 years
  • Normally 12-16 inches in height
  • Females weigh 49-51 lbs, males 53-55 lbs
  • Originated in England
  • Docile and friendly tempermant
